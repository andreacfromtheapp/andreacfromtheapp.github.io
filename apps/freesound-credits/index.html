Command line utility to credit Freesound samples in a usable markdown file

A command line utility to help you credit Freesound samples for any given project.

Why freesound-credits

Giving credits in the Creative Commons community is often the sole requirement to freely use samples in your own creations. This tool helps you abide to Copyleft and credit the generous artists whom contribute to Freesound, helping your creativity.

Until today, giving credits may have been a cumbersome and tedious process that you begrudged. This may have been stopping you from giving credits altogether.

Not anymore! Thanks to freesound-credits it takes seconds now!
